BMW Group Internship: EINK

I had a 20 Week Internship at BMW in the Driving Simulation Center in Munich, working on a daily basis with the Unreal Engine 5, C++ on a complex project in a Computergraphics Topic, in regards to the EINK Topic from ground up.

I gained more Experience with working with MeshData, Matrix Calculations / custom Projection on Uneven Surfaces, and designing a Complex Software Architecture.

Bipedal locomotion: humanoid skeletal model

I am working with Unreal Engine on the generalization of my motion pattern generator towards controlled walking, running, and interaction with the terrain.

Pathfinding: A* Algorithm

I developed a pathfinding solution based on the A* Algorithm for 3D environments, utilizing a 2D grid map to store nodes. These nodes are either pre-collected from meshes at the start of the game or dynamically added asynchronously during gameplay. This ensures that a route, such as one guiding a bot to the player, is always successfully determined.

Terrain generation: Bezier splines

Terrain generation using connected Bezier splines, including generation of simple trees and their meshes.
